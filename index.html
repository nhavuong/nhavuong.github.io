WE'RE GONA MAKE DREAMS COMES

My best little brother. Will make every good things for him! I chose my current major in Software Engineering because it was where I felt like I could best apply my skills in designing computers and mobile applications and other types of software for a large array of people, especially those with disabilities. I believe this is partially motivated by my brother who was born prematurely at 7 months due to complicated health conditions. He was not able to communicate himself through the avenues of reading or writing, but due to advancements in technology, he is now able to use mobile and computer devices by voice and signal controlling in apps that help him communicate through other means. For this reason, I was drawn to technology and science and specifically computer science. I realized that Computer Science would be me closer to my dream of making a better life for my brother and everyone like him.
